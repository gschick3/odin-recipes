Corn and Zucchini Queso Chowder

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 cup finely chopped onion
  • 3 poblano peppers, seeded and finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups frozen whole kernel corn, thawed
  • 2 cups chopped zucchini
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2-3 potatoes, cubed
  • 2 (14-oz.) cans low-sodium fat-free chicken broth
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 whole milk
  • 1 cup freshly shredded asadero or montery jack cheese
  • 1 cup freshly shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Toppings: cooked bacon, shredded Cheddar cheese, diced red onion

Instructions

  1. Melt butter over medium-high heat. Add bell pepper, onion, poblano peppers, and garlic; sauté 4 to 5 minutes or until tender. Add corn, zucchini, and cumin, and sauté 5 minutes or until tender
  2. Gradually pour in broth, stirring to loosen particles from bottom of pot. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer 15 minutes.
  3. Whisk together flour and whole milk. Stir into corn mixture, and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, for 5 minutes or until thickened.
  4. Add cheeses and salt, stirring constantly until cheeses melt and mixture is thoroughly heated. Serve with desired toppings.
